About Us
Rustamji Institute of Technology (RJIT) is the first engineering college established by Paramilitary Force having a history of 25 years, affiliated by RGPV Bhopal.
The Institute is named after the founding father of BSF Padma Vibhushan late K.F Rustamji. The aim for setting up the institute was to provide the technical education for the children of BSF personnels who are in military. The institute encourages the message of "आत्म दीपो भवः", "Be Your Own Light"
